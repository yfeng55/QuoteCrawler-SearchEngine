Donald Trump ‘Will Get Crushed in the Fall,’ John Kasich Says

Donald Trump “will get crushed in the fall,” said one of his remaining rivals for the Republican nomination, Ohio Governor John Kasich, who cited protests outside the California Republican Party convention that hindered Trump’s entrance to the event.

  • Kasich, speaking to reporters at the convention near San Francisco, doubled down on his argument that he’s best positioned to take on Hillary Clinton in a general election
  • "His best day is going to be the day he walks in, and from there his support will decline,’’ Kasich said of Trump
  • Kasich stopped short of saying he’d win California, which a Republican hasn’t carried in a presidential election since 1988, but said he’d compete strongly in Michigan, Wisconsin, Minnesota and Pennsylvania
